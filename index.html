Quiz App

The simplest way to give your class a quiz.

This app is currently in development by the PFMS Coders and is available on GitHub here.

Features

  • Beautiful and responsive UI
  • Download quiz results in a PDF
  • Flexible question creation options
  • Easy to share quizzes with your class
  • See results in real-time as your students finish your quiz
  • Works with any computer and most mobile devices
  • It's completely free
